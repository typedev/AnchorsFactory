# Centroid

`outline.centroid` is the area centre of mass of the outline — the glyph's
optical centre, a single 2-D point usable in either the X or the Y slot. This
chapter shows where it beats box/scanline positions: lopsided base letters
(centring a diacritic over the visual mass of `a`), and enclosing or overlay
marks that want both axes at once.
