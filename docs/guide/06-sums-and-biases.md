# Sums & biases

Any position can be a sum of terms joined with `+` and `-` (written without
spaces): on Y this combines heights (`capHeight*1/2+xHeight*1/2` is their
midpoint), on X it adds a bias to a base position (`outline.centroid-25`
nudges an acute toward the letter's foot). This chapter covers what may appear
as a term (numbers, metrics, `$glyph`, `&variables`, other positions), and the
two caveats: hyphenated glyph names in sums, and `@` owning the rest of its
token.
