# X positions

Horizontal positions come from one of three frames: `width` (the advance),
`box` (the bounding box) and `outline` (the actual ink, measured on a
horizontal scanline). This chapter covers alignment words (`left`/`center`/
`right`), fractional positions (`width*1/3`), picking a specific ink span with
runs (`outline.2.center` for the middle stem of `m`), and decoupling the
sample height from the anchor's Y with `@` (`outline.center@xHeight`).
